About Me

I am a rising senior from Atlanta, GA at Duke University majoring in Statistical Science with minors in Math and Computer Science. I am interested in big data, data science, machine learning and their applications, particularly in sports analytics.

I am currently the co-director for Project Arts, an arts-related pre-orientation program offered for incoming first-year students. Additionally, I am involved with Hoof 'n' Horn, and have been the Assistant Music Director and Rehearsal Pianist for a couple of shows.

In my free time, I enjoy playing the piano and discovering new online board games (since we're all currently in quarantine). In addition, I am an avid soccer fan and a huge Liverpool supporter.
