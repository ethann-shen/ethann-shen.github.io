About Me

I am a rising senior from Atlanta, GA at Duke University. I am majoring in Statistical Science with minors in Math and Computer. I am also currently the co-director for Project Arts, an arts-related pre-orientation program offered for incoming first-year students. Additionally, I am also involved with Hoof 'n' Horn, and have been the Assistant Music Director and Rehearsal Pianist for a couple of shows.

In my free time, I enjoy playing the piano and discovering new online board games (since we're all in quarantine). In addition, I am an avid soccer fan and a huge Liverpool fan.
